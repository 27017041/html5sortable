Angular example with connected lists

How to use

Connect two lists by passing the connectWith option to the html-sortable directive:

        <ul html-sortable="{ connectWith: '.connected' }" class="connected" ng-model='data1'>...</ul>
        <ul html-sortable="{ connectWith: '.connected' }" class="connected" ng-model='data2'>...</ul>
